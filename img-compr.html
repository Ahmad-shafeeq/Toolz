How to Compress Images Online

Our free image compressor helps you reduce image file size while maintaining good quality. Here's how it works:

  1. Upload your image - Drag and drop your JPG, PNG, or WebP file into the upload area
  2. Adjust settings - Choose your desired compression level and output format
  3. Compress - Click the "Compress Image" button to process your file
  4. Download - Save your optimized image to your device

Why Compress Images?

Image compression is essential for:

  • Faster website loading times
  • Reduced bandwidth usage
  • Improved SEO rankings (Google considers page speed)
  • Saving storage space on your devices
  • Sharing images more easily via email or messaging
